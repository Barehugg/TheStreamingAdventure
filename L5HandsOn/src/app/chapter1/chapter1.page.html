Chapter 1 - About Me and My Start

  My name is Barehugg, this book is to tell you a little bit about streaming, what it takes, and what to expect to get out of it. I started streaming in 2020 during the covid pandemic and have grown very fast ever since. I am a 100% disabled veteran but at the time was not rated at 100% yet, I had been laid off from work with no hope to return, we lost our house and alost lost everything.
  I served in the military from 2011-2016 as a GSE(Gas Turbine Systems Electrician) in the US Navy. I have done many jobs that really put a strain on my body as it was already broken and weak from my military service, so finding another job that I could do with my background and that would be easy on my body was close to impossible, so my wife encouraged me to start streaming, even if i only made $20 a month, that would help us pay for diapers and I already played the games anyways.
  So my streaming adventure started, it started off with reaching out to everyone around, asking friends and family for help to get it off the ground. I did tons of research up to 16 hours some days just figuring out what I could do better and what I could improve on to try to make it the best stream possible. All of this research and work I then passed on to others that asked or wanted help to try to help everyone as a whole, try to grow a community around helping one another and building each-other up.
  When I started streaming I had a 10 year old computer, no screen I used a $20 TV with a stand made of wood I made, a headset that was duck-taped together, a mouse that did not scroll, and a keyboard with broken keys. After 1 year of streaming my community helped me afford all the things needed to give them a better experience and an entire computer set up that allows me to give them the best experience possible. Now I just want to pay it forward and pass the love onto others and reach out to help as many people out there as I can.
  This led to having a very amazing community that loves helping one another and just wants to spread the love and joy to all that we meet, it took a lot of time and work to meet these people and to grow to what it is today, but it was 100% worth it and quite the adventure that I am very glad that I took. So if any of this information helps you or brings you joy or peace, just tell my wife and my community thank you!
